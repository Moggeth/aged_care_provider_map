Aged care service list: 30 June 2025

Author:
Department of Health, Disability and Ageing
Released:
07 October 2025
Topic:
Providers, services and places in aged care
Series:
Aged care service list
Summary

Information on aged care services subsidised by the Australian Government under the Aged Care Act 1997, including:

  • Residential aged care services (aged care homes)
  • Services that provide Home Care Packages 
  • Transition Care
  • Innovation Pool
  • Multi-Purpose Services providing aged care
  • Short Term Restorative Care.

It also includes services funded under the National Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Flexible Aged Care Program.

The files are current as at 30 June 2025 and are updated annually. This release has been updated on 21/11/2025 to include service-level funding data. 

Correction:

  • 10/10/2025 – Aged Care Planning Region (ACPR) columns were mislabelled as 2019. Both PDF and XLSX versions of these files been corrected to 2018.
  • 02/02/2026 – The NSW, QLD, SA, WA and Australia files have been updated. A small number of Multi-Purpose services had incorrect service name data, which has now been corrected as noted below:
    • NSW: Walcha Multi-Purpose Service, Warialda Multi-Purpose Service, Tocumwal Multi-Purpose Service, Walgett Multi-Purpose Service, Warren Multi-Purpose Service, Boorowa Multi-Purpose Service
    • QLD: Jandowae Multi-Purpose Service, Blackwater Multi-Purpose Service
    • SA: Waikerie Multi-Purpose Service
    • WA: Dalwallinu Multi-Purpose Service, Quairading Multi-Purpose Service (suburb correction).
